    <section id="uk-fhi-aims-user-and-developer-meeting-2024">
<h1>UK FHI-aims User and Developer Meeting 2024<a class="headerlink" href="#uk-fhi-aims-user-and-developer-meeting-2024" title="Permalink to this heading">¶</a></h1>
<div class="toctree-wrapper compound">
</div>
<section id="overview">
<h2>Overview<a class="headerlink" href="#overview" title="Permalink to this heading">¶</a></h2>
<p>The FHI-aims User and Developer Meeting will bring together the userbase of FHI-aims with the code developers to explore and discuss the impacts and uses of FHI-aims
within institutions in the UK as well as exhibiting new features and recent developments.</p>
</section>
<section id="time-and-place">
<h2>Time and Place<a class="headerlink" href="#time-and-place" title="Permalink to this heading">¶</a></h2>
<p>The meeting is being hosted by the <a class="reference external" href="https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/chemistry/research/maurer/maurergroup/">Maurer Group</a> at <a class="reference external" href="https://warwick.ac.uk/">The University of Warwick</a> from the 15th to the 16th of May 2024.
The venue is the Science Concourse within the Physics Department. Directions can be found on this <a class="reference external" href="https://campus.warwick.ac.uk/">interactive campus map</a>.</p>
</section>
<section id="accommodation-and-travel">
<h2>Accommodation and Travel<a class="headerlink" href="#accommodation-and-travel" title="Permalink to this heading">¶</a></h2>
<p>There will be a limited number of rooms available to be booked on the university campus for attendees. Other accommodation is available within Coventry city, a short bus ride
from the campus.</p>
<p>Travel to the University of Warwick is achieved easily through rail connections to Coventry station and then a bus journey from outside the station to the campus.
More information about how to get to the university can be found on the <a class="reference external" href="https://warwick.ac.uk/about/visiting/directions/">university website</a>.</p>
</section>
<section id="tutorials-and-hack">
<h2>Tutorials and Hack<a class="headerlink" href="#tutorials-and-hack" title="Permalink to this heading">¶</a></h2>
<p>This meeting will include a number of tutorials before the main sessions of talks. The topics of these tutorials are ultimately to be driven by what attendees wish to
see tutorials on however there will be a session on the fundamentals of FHI-aims and hwo best to utilise the software. Other, more advanced tutorials will be available and
will be published here in due course.</p>
<p>After the closing of the main body of the meeting, all attendees are invited to a code hack, wherein the developers of the codebase will get together and take part in some
collaborative coding. The organisers would like to invite anyone with a suggestion for features to be included in FHI-aims to write these into the appropriate box during
the registration process.</p>
</section>
<section id="registration">
<h2>Registration<a class="headerlink" href="#registration" title="Permalink to this heading">¶</a></h2>
<p>If you plan to attend the meeting then please <a class="reference external" href="https://null">register here</a> so that the correct catering and accessibility requirements can be met.
Abstracts for both posters and talks are welcomed and can be submitted during the registration process. There is no fee for registration.
The deadline for registration and abstract submission is 24/04/2024. Once registered, your place at the meeting will be confirmed by the
organising committee after this date.</p>
</section>
